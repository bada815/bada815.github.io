# How to Manually Deploy Your Notion Blog

If you want your changes to appear immediately without waiting for the automatic schedule (every 6 hours), you can trigger the deployment manually.

## Steps

1.  Go to the **Actions** tab in your GitHub repository.
2.  Click on the **"Deploy Notion Site to GitHub Pages"** workflow in the left sidebar.
3.  Click the **Run workflow** dropdown button on the right side.
4.  Click the green **Run workflow** button.

![Manual Deployment Steps](https://docs.github.com/assets/cb-25535/images/help/actions/manually-running-a-workflow.png)
*(Example image from GitHub Docs)*

## Automatic Deployment
Your site is also configured to automatically check for updates and deploy **every 6 hours**.
